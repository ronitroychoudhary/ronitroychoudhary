## About Me

I am a Computational Biologist and Machine Learning Researcher specializing in 3D genomics, transcriptional regulation, and multi-omics data integration. My research focuses on developing deep learning architectures to predict chromatin conformation maps from sequence and transcription signals.

* **Research Focus**: Developing deep learning models for multi-condition Hi-C contact map prediction from DNA sequence and RNA-seq data.
* **Collaborations**: Seeking collaborations on multi-omics integration, spatial transcriptomics, and cancer transcriptomics.
* **Technical Interests**: Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) for heterogeneous biomedical data fusion.
* **Domain Expertise**: RNA-seq analysis, Hi-C processing pipelines, survival modeling, and biomarker discovery.
* **Current Learning**: Deep learning frameworks for multi-omics disease subtyping and single-cell conformation modeling.
* **Key Finding**: Identified 133 differentially expressed genes (DEGs) conserved across three continents in oral squamous cell carcinoma (tongue cancer).
